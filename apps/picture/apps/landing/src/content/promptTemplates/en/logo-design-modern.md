## Professional Logo Design with AI

Ideogram V3 Turbo is the best model for logo design thanks to its exceptional text rendering capabilities. This template helps you create professional, memorable logos that work across all mediums.

### Key Principles

1. **Simplicity** - Best logos are simple and memorable
2. **Versatility** - Works in color and monochrome
3. **Scalability** - Looks good at any size
4. **Timelessness** - Avoids trendy elements that date quickly

### Logo Types Supported

- **Wordmark** - Text-only logos
- **Lettermark** - Initial-based logos
- **Icon + Text** - Combined symbol and text
- **Badge** - Circular or shield-shaped
- **Abstract** - Geometric or abstract symbols
