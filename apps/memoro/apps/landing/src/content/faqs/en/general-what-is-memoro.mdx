Memoro revolutionizes how you capture information:

- **Speak instead of type**: Just record and let AI do the rest
- **Intelligent structuring**: Automatic organization and summarization
- **Instantly searchable**: All recordings are fully searchable
- **Multi-platform**: Available for iOS, Android, Web, and Desktop
