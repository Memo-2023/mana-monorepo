Memoro is designed to work seamlessly across all major platforms, ensuring you can access your notes and conversations wherever you work. Our desktop applications support macOS, Windows, and Linux, while our mobile apps are available for both iOS and Android devices. All platforms sync automatically when connected to the internet, allowing you to switch between devices without missing a beat.
