Felix Wolkenstein is the designer who makes Memoro not just functional, but beautiful. As Lead UX/UI Designer, he creates the spaces where our users think, work, and be creative.

## The Creative Half of the Wolkenstein Brothers

While his older brother Oliver builds Memoro's technical infrastructure, Felix takes care of what users see and feel. This unique sibling dynamic is a real asset to the team: Felix has a deep understanding of technical possibilities, Oliver has a trained eye for good design.

"We complement each other perfectly," Felix laughs. "Oliver explains what's technically possible, and I show him how it should look. Sometimes we argue, but in the end, something great always emerges."

## From Sketchbook to Screen

Felix's design journey began in childhood - while Oliver wrote his first programs, Felix designed fantastic worlds on paper. After studying Visual Communication at UdK Berlin and working at renowned design agencies, he found his true calling at Memoro.

## Design with Soul

Felix's design philosophy is clear:
- **People before pixels**: Every design starts with "How does this feel for the user?"
- **Clarity through reduction**: Removing is harder than adding
- **Consistency is king**: The "Cumulus" design system is his masterpiece
- **Beauty with purpose**: Aesthetics always serve function

## The Felix Touch at Memoro

His designs are instantly recognizable:
- **Flowing transitions**: Nothing jumps, everything flows
- **Thoughtful color palette**: Calming but not boring
- **Micro-interactions**: Small details that bring joy
- **Perfect dark mode**: His personal pride
- **Accessibility first**: Beautiful for everyone, usable by everyone

## The Process

Felix's working method is methodical yet creative:
1. **Research**: Hours of user observation
2. **Sketches**: Always start with pen and paper
3. **Prototyping**: Test quickly, learn quickly
4. **Iteration**: "Good enough" doesn't exist
5. **Celebration**: Celebrate successes (with the team)

With Felix's creative vision and eye for detail, Memoro becomes more than just a tool - it becomes a daily companion you love to use.
