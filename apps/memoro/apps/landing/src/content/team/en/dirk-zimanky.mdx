Dirk Zimanky is an experienced entrepreneur and strategic advisor with over 30 years of expertise in the global electronics industry. As founder of adCura, he advises owners, investors, and boards on increasing enterprise value. At Memoro, he brings his extensive experience as a mentor and strategic advisor.

## From Global Executive to Startup Mentor

"I work every day to make people and organizations successful that have a positive impact on our environment and socio-economic life," describes Dirk his mission. With his impressive track record - from the management buy-out at Enics to leading as CEO to building adCura - he brings invaluable experience to Memoro's growth strategy.

His career includes founding and leading Enics (2004), where as Co-Founder and later President & CEO he grew the company from EUR 116 million to over EUR 500 million in annual revenue. After his time at Enics, he founded adCura in 2020, a consulting firm specializing in enterprise value creation.

## Strategic Expertise for Memoro

### International Expansion
With his broad international network and experience in Asia, Europe, and the USA, Dirk supports Memoro in building strategic partnerships and global market development. His expertise in multicultural contract negotiations is invaluable.

### Business Development
Dirk brings his core competencies:
- Building long-term strategic partnerships
- Evaluating and optimizing organizational structures
- Motivating teams toward challenging goals
- Mergers and acquisitions on both buy and sell side

### Knowledge Management & AI
As Board Member at edisconet and through his collaboration with HSLU Lucerne University in AI-powered knowledge management, Dirk brings valuable insights to Memoro's product development. His vision: "Generative AI can help make hidden knowledge in organizations visible - exactly what Memoro delivers for its users."
