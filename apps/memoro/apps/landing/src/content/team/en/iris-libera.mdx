Iris Libera is the designer who makes Memoro not just user-friendly, but touching. As Senior UX/UI Designer, she understands how to shape the emotional connection between humans and technology.

## Design with Depth

"Good design is invisible, great design touches the soul," is Iris's credo. With her unique background in psychology and design, she brings a special perspective to every project: She doesn't just design how something looks, but how it feels.

Her education - a Bachelor's in Psychology followed by a Master's in Communication Design - was no accident. "I wanted to understand how people think and feel, to then create designs that truly resonate."

## The Path to Memoro

After years at a renowned Berlin design agency and formative time at Spotify, where she helped shape emotional design for personalized playlists, Iris found her true calling at Memoro.

"Memoro is more than an app - it's a companion for the most intimate moments of thinking and reflecting. This responsibility drives me to give my best every day."

## The Iris Method

Her approach is as unique as it is effective:

### Emotional Design Framework
- **Empathy First**: Every design begins with real understanding
- **Color Psychology**: Colors as emotional guides
- **Micro-Delights**: Small moments of joy in the interface
- **Inclusive by Design**: Accessibility is not an afterthought

### Special Projects at Memoro
- **Calm Mode**: A soothing mode for stressful days
- **Emotional Onboarding**: Gently guides new users
- **Synesthetic Themes**: Color schemes based on moods
- **Accessibility Excellence**: WCAG AAA in all areas

## The Design Duo: Iris & Felix

The collaboration with Felix Wolkenstein is a creative dance: "Felix builds the house, I make it a home," Iris describes their partnership. While Felix creates structural elegance, Iris breathes life and emotion into the design.

Their joint brainstorming sessions are legendary - post-its in all colors, endless sketches, and profound discussions about user psychology.

## Tools & Inspiration

## The Human Side

Away from screens:
- **Art Therapy**: Training to be a certified therapist
- **Synesthesia**: Sees music in colors (shapes her design)
- **Poetry Slams**: Shares her thoughts on stage
- **Meditation**: Daily practice for inner peace
- **Social Engagement**: Teaches design in underprivileged schools

## Her Vision

"Technology should feel like a warm hug, not a cold handshake. At Memoro, I design interfaces that people don't just use, but love. That understand, support, and inspire them. That's design that makes a difference."

With her rare combination of psychological depth, artistic sensitivity, and technical understanding, Iris makes Memoro an app that not only works but reaches users' hearts.
