Emma Lichtblick is far more than just Head of Customer Success - she is the soul of Memoro's user support. Her name says it all: For many users, she is literally the "ray of light" (Lichtblick) when they need help.

## The Art of Helping

"Support is not a department, it's an attitude," Emma likes to say. She lives this philosophy every day. With over 6 years of experience in Customer Success, she has learned: The best solutions emerge when you really listen.

Her career took her from telecommunications call centers through HubSpot and Zendesk to Memoro. At each step, she gathered valuable insights about what makes excellent customer service.

## The Emma Effect

Colleagues speak of the "Emma Effect": After a conversation with her, everyone feels better - whether frustrated users or stressed team members. This special gift has measurable results:

- **98% customer satisfaction** - Record high since company founding
- **2-hour average response time** - even for complex requests
- **60% faster onboarding** - through her revolutionary welcome program
- **85% retention rate** for enterprise customers

## More Than Just Support

Emma understands Customer Success as a holistic task:

### Proactive Care
Instead of waiting for problems, Emma actively reaches out to users. Her "Health Check" system identifies users who might need support before they become frustrated.

### Community Building
Under her leadership, the Memoro forum became a vibrant community with over 5,000 active members. "Users helping users" - but Emma is always there when it matters.

### Education Over Operation
Her video tutorials and webinars are legendary. "Give a person a fish, and they eat for a day. Teach them to fish..." - Emma teaches digital fishing.

## The Method Behind the Smile

Emma's recipe for success:
1. **Empathy first**: Every problem has a human side
2. **Clarity wins**: Translate complex solutions into simple steps
3. **Celebration allowed**: Acknowledge every user success
4. **Feedback loop**: User input = product gold
