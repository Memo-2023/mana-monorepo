Tobias Müller is co-founder and the technical soul of Memoro. As a full-stack developer with a soft spot for everything new and innovative, he has been instrumental in developing Memoro from an idea into a functioning platform. After intense years of building, he is currently taking a well-deserved sabbatical.

## The Developer with Founder Spirit

"Expand your thinking" - this motto shapes Tobias' approach to technology and life. As a full-stack developer, he defines himself not just through code, but through the passion to push boundaries and explore new paths. His two life principles: "Always broaden your horizon" and "Always go one step further" are reflected in every project he tackles.

With his Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering (specializing in Games Engineering) from Heilbronn University, Tobias brings a solid technical foundation. But it's his independent thinking and entrepreneurial spirit that truly set him apart.

## From Idea to Innovation

### The Memoro Story
In June 2023, Tobias co-founded Memoro with Till Schneider. What began as a vision to intelligently document conversations became an innovative platform under his technical leadership. As founder, he was not just a developer, but also a visionary, problem solver, and team builder.

"One year of Memoro and 32 years of Tobi!" - Celebrating his birthday together with Memoro's first anniversary perfectly symbolizes how closely his personal journey is intertwined with the company. In the best company, surrounded by favorite people, they not only celebrated but also reaffirmed the vision: creating more space for what matters most in life.
