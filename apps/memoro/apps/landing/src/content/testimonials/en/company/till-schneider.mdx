## Memoro as Thought Storage

As CEO of Memoro, I use our app daily as an external thought storage. Every spontaneous idea, every important thought, every strategic consideration - everything is captured immediately. Memoro acts like a second brain that never forgets.

## The Company Memory

For our company, Memoro has become an indispensable knowledge repository. All important information about:
- **Product decisions** and their rationale
- **Customer feedback** from hundreds of conversations
- **Strategic considerations** and pivots
- **Team discussions** and creative brainstorming sessions

Everything is searchable, structured, and available forever.

## The True Value

- **No knowledge is lost** - every piece of information is preserved
- **Fast decisions** through instant access to all relevant information
- **Continuous learning** from past conversations and ideas
- **Perfect handovers** for new team members

Memoro is not just an app - it's the collective memory of our company that grows and learns with us.
