# Team Meeting & Work Planning

This blueprint optimizes team communication and ensures efficient workflows in craft businesses.

## Key Features

### Work Allocation
Clear assignment of tasks and responsibilities to team members with timeframes.

### Project Coordination
Overview of ongoing projects, priorities, and resource distribution.

### Process Optimization
Collection of improvement suggestions and best practices from the team.

### Clarify Responsibilities
Clear documentation of responsibilities and contact persons.

## Used Prompts

1. **Executive Summary**: Compact weekly planning
2. **Tasks & Appointments**: Work allocation, deadlines, material preparation
3. **Ideas & Suggestions**: Process improvements and solution approaches
4. **Open Questions**: Clarification needed with clients or suppliers

## Use Cases

- Weekly team meetings
- Daily work allocation
- Project kickoff meetings
- Debriefings
- Monthly planning
- Emergency meetings

## Tips for Optimal Results

1. Start with a brief status round from all team members
2. Visualize the weekly planning for everyone to see
3. Prioritize tasks together as a team
4. Clarify material availability in advance
5. Document obstacles and solutions
6. Agree on clear deadlines and milestones
7. Plan buffer times for unforeseen events
