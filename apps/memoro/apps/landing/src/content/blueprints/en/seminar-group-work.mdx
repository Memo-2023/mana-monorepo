# Seminar & Group Work

This blueprint optimizes collaboration in seminars and group work through structured capture of all important information.

## Key Features

### Task Management
Clear capture of tasks with responsibilities and deadlines for all group members.

### Idea Collection
Structured brainstorming and collection of creative approaches for joint projects.

### Result Documentation
Compact summary of the main results of each discussion for all participants.

### Open Points
Documentation of unresolved questions for the next session or further research.

## Used Prompts

1. **Tasks & Appointments**: With responsibilities and deadlines
2. **Ideas & Suggestions**: Brainstorming and creative approaches
3. **Executive Summary**: Main results of the discussion
4. **Open Questions**: For the next session

## Use Cases

- Seminar discussions
- Group work
- Project meetings
- Brainstorming sessions
- Presentation preparation
- Collaborative research

## Tips for Optimal Results

1. Establish discussion rules at the beginning
2. Appoint a moderator
3. Place the device in the center of the table
4. Clearly state names when assigning tasks
5. Repeat important agreements
6. Summarize next steps at the end
7. Share the summary with all participants
