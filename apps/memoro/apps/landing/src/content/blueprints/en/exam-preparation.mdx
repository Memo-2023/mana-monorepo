# Exam Preparation

This blueprint is optimal for intensive exam preparation and transforms study material into effective learning aids.

## Key Features

### Q&A System
Automatic creation of question-answer pairs for flashcards and self-tests.

### Quick Review
Compact summaries for last-minute review before the exam.

### Detailed Study Material
Comprehensive documentation of all exam-relevant topics with context.

### Knowledge Gap Identification
Automatic recognition of areas that need further deepening.

## Used Prompts

1. **Q&A**: Perfect for flashcards and self-test
2. **Executive Summary**: Quick review before the exam
3. **Detailed Summary**: Detailed exam material
4. **Open Questions**: Identifies knowledge gaps

## Use Cases

- Study groups
- Self-study
- Exam simulation
- Review courses
- Past exam discussions
- Oral exam preparation

## Tips for Optimal Results

1. Speak study material out loud
2. Formulate your own questions while studying
3. Explain complex concepts in your own words
4. Use the Pomodoro technique with recording breaks
5. Repeat important definitions multiple times
6. Create mnemonics and record them
7. Test yourself with the generated questions
