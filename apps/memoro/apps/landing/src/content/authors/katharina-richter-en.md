# About Dr. Katharina Richter

Dr. Katharina Richter is Memoro's legal guardian. As Legal Counsel and Data Protection Officer, she ensures that innovation and legal security go hand in hand.

## Law as Innovation Driver

With her PhD in IT Law (summa cum laude) and over 15 years of experience in the tech industry, Katharina understands that good legal management is not a brake but an accelerator for growth.

After positions at an international law firm and as Head of Legal at a successful FinTech startup, she joined Memoro: "An app that processes such sensitive data as conversations needs the highest legal standards. This isn't an obligation, it's our responsibility to users."

## Legal Excellence at Memoro

Katharina's work is based on three pillars:

### 1. Privacy by Design
- Privacy considered from the start
- Encryption as standard
- Minimal data collection
- Transparent data processing

### 2. Compliance as Competitive Advantage
- GDPR Gold Standard
- ISO 27001 certification targeted
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Regular audits

### 3. Enabling Innovation
- Legally secure frameworks for new features
- Proactive risk analysis
- Fast, pragmatic solutions
- "Legal as a Service" for all teams
