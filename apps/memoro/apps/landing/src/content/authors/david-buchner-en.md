# About David Buchner

David is the architect of Memoro's growth strategy. As Business Development Manager, he opens doors, builds bridges, and creates win-win situations for everyone involved.

## The Relationship Manager

With a degree in Business Administration (focus on International Business) and over 10 years of B2B sales experience, David has developed a keen sense for successful business relationships. His career led him through various SaaS companies where he consistently broke revenue records and built sustainable partnerships.

"At Memoro, it's not just about selling software. We help companies transform their communication culture. That's a mission I stand behind 100%."

David's approach is based on four pillars:

### 1. Relationships Before Transactions
- Long-term partnerships instead of quick wins
- Trust as the foundation of every business relationship
- Personal contact in digital times

### 2. Mutual Value Creation
- Identifying win-win situations
- Defining shared success criteria
- Partnerships that grow together

### 3. Strategic Thinking
- Recognizing market trends early
- Opening new sales channels
- Driving international expansion

### 4. Customer Success Integration
- Close collaboration with Emma (Customer Success)
- Feedback loops between Sales and Product
- Customer success as top priority
