# The Memoro Team

We are an interdisciplinary team of developers, designers, content creators, and community managers. Our common goal is to make Memoro the best platform for personal knowledge management.

## Our Mission

- **User-friendliness**: We develop intuitive features that everyone can use
- **Innovation**: We meaningfully integrate the latest AI technologies into our platform
- **Community**: We listen to our users' feedback and develop Memoro together

- Technical documentation
