# About Iris Libera

Iris is the soul behind Memoro's emotional design. As a Senior UX/UI Designer, she masterfully bridges functionality and feeling, making every interaction with Memoro a meaningful experience.

## The Psychology of Design

With a background in Psychology (B.A.) and Communication Design (M.A.), Iris brings a unique perspective to the design team. "Design is applied psychology," she often says. "We don't just design interfaces, we design feelings, habits, and thought processes."

Her career took her from a Berlin design agency through Spotify (where she worked on emotional design for playlists) to Memoro, where she can fully live out her vision of empathetic design.

## Design Philosophy

Iris's approach is profound and human-centered:

1. **Emotion before function**: "Users remember how they felt"
2. **Inclusion as standard**: Design for everyone, not just the majority
3. **Storytelling through design**: Every screen tells a story
4. **Ethical design**: Responsibility for psychological impact

## Her Signature at Memoro

- **Emotional color palette**: Colors that evoke feelings and support moods
- **Micro-animations with soul**: Movements that show personality
- **User journey mapping**: Understands every emotional touchpoint
- **Accessibility excellence**: Accessibility is her passion
- **Design meditation**: Calm, focused interfaces for deep work

## Collaboration with Felix

As a design duo, Iris and Felix complement each other perfectly: While Felix creates the visual structure, Iris breathes life into it. "Felix is the architect, I'm the interior designer," she describes their collaboration. Their joint design sessions are legendary - a mix of creative explosions and profound discussions.

## Methods & Processes

- **Empathy mapping**: Deep understanding of user emotions
- **Design psychology workshops**: Training the team in user psychology
- **Color psychology studies**: How colors influence productivity
- **Inclusive design audits**: Regular accessibility reviews

## Achievements

- **Emotional onboarding**: 40% higher completion rate through emotional guidance
- **Calm mode**: A special mode for anxious or stressed users
- **Inclusive color system**: Works for all types of color blindness
- **Micro-delight library**: Over 50 small moments of joy in the interface

## Personal Side

Outside of Memoro:

- **Art therapist in training**: Combines creativity with healing
- **Synesthete**: Sees music in colors (inspires her design)
- **Meditation & mindfulness**: Practices daily
- **Poetry slams**: Performs regularly
- **Volunteer work**: Teaches design in social projects

## Her Vision

"At Memoro, I don't just design beautiful surfaces - I create spaces where people can be themselves. Where technology doesn't intimidate but invites. Where every click feels right. That's design that touches the soul."

With her unique combination of psychological understanding, artistic talent, and deep empathy, Iris makes Memoro more than an app - a trusted companion.
