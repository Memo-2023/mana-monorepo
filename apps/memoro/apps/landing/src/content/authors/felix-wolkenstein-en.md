# About Felix Wolkenstein

Felix is the creative mind behind Memoro's visual identity. As Lead UX/UI Designer, he transforms complex features into intuitive, beautiful experiences that users love to use every day.

## The Wolkenstein Brothers

As the younger brother of Oliver (our Senior Software Engineer), Felix brings a unique perspective to the team. While Oliver builds the technical architecture, Felix designs the interface users interact with. "We're like Yin and Yang," says Felix. "Oliver makes sure everything works, I make sure it feels right."

This sibling dynamic leads to exceptional results: Felix understands technical possibilities and limitations, while Oliver has developed an eye for good design.

## The Path to Design

Felix's passion for design began early - while Oliver wrote code, Felix sketched user interfaces on paper. After studying Visual Communication at UdK Berlin, he worked at various design agencies before joining Memoro.

"When Oliver told me about Memoro, I knew immediately: This is it. A product where design really makes a difference. Where every pixel counts because it helps people organize their thoughts."

## Design Philosophy

Felix's approach to design:

1. **Empathy before aesthetics**: "Beauty without function is just decoration"
2. **Less is more**: Every element must have its purpose
3. **Consistency creates trust**: The design system is sacred
4. **Accessibility is not a feature**: It's a basic requirement

## Achievements at Memoro

- **Design System "Cumulus"**: Named after clouds (Wolkenstein!), unifies all UI elements
- **95% Usability Score**: Consistently high ratings in user tests
- **Dark Mode**: His "baby" - perfectly balanced for long work sessions
- **Micro-Interactions**: Small animations that bring Memoro to life
- **Accessibility First**: WCAG AAA compliant in all core features

## Working Style

Felix is known for:
- His "Design Critique Thursdays" (with donuts!)
- Endless iterations until perfection
- User testing sessions where he watches himself
- The ability to get developers excited about design
- His inspiring mood boards

## His Vision

"At Memoro, I'm not just designing interfaces - I'm designing thinking spaces. Every screen, every interaction should feel like a deep breath. Clear, calm, focused. When users say 'Memoro just feels right,' then I know: Mission accomplished."

With his blend of technical understanding (Wolkenstein genes!), creative talent, and user empathy, Felix makes Memoro not just functional, but beautiful too.
