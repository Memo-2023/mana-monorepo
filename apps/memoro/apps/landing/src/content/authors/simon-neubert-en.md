# About Dr. Simon Neubert

Dr. Simon Neubert is the mastermind behind Memoro's AI technology. As Data Scientist and AI Engineer, he develops the intelligent systems that transform conversations into structured knowledge.

## Academic Background

After studying Computer Science at TU Munich, Simon earned his PhD at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems in Stuttgart. His dissertation on "Contextual Language Processing in Multilingual Environments" laid the foundation for Memoro's core functions.

"During my research, I realized: We need AI that understands not just words, but their meaning in context. At Memoro, I can bring this vision to life."

## The Science Behind Memoro

Simon's work focuses on:

### 1. Intelligent Conversation Analysis

- **Real-time transcription**: Precise speech recognition in 15+ languages
- **Context understanding**: AI that recognizes connections
- **Topic extraction**: Automatic identification of core themes
- **Sentiment analysis**: Recognition of emotional nuances

### 2. Ethical AI Development

- **Privacy by design**: Encryption and anonymization
- **Bias reduction**: Fair and inclusive algorithms
- **Transparency**: Explainable AI decisions
- **User control**: Full data sovereignty for users

### 3. Innovation & Research

- **Transformer architectures**: State-of-the-art language models
- **Few-shot learning**: Quick adaptation to new domains
- **Multimodal integration**: Text, audio, and visual data
- **Edge AI**: Local processing for maximum privacy

## Technical Achievements

- **95% accuracy**: In speech recognition in quiet environments
- **Sub-second latency**: Real-time processing without noticeable delay
- **60% time savings**: Through automatic summaries
- **GDPR compliant**: Full compliance with data protection standards
