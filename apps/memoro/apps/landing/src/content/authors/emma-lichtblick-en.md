# About Emma Lichtblick

Emma is the heart of our Customer Success team. With her unique ability to transform complex problems into simple solutions, she is many users' first "ray of light" (Lichtblick) when facing challenges with Memoro.

## The Path to Customer Success

Emma's career began in customer service at a major telecommunications company, where she quickly realized: "Support isn't just about solving problems - it's the art of understanding people." After positions at HubSpot (Customer Success Manager) and Zendesk (Head of Customer Experience DACH), she brings a wealth of experience to Memoro.

"At Memoro, I found my calling. Here I can not only help people with technical questions but show them how to better organize their thoughts and ideas. That changes lives!"

## Achievements at Memoro

- **98% Customer Satisfaction**: Highest rating in company history
- **Onboarding Revolution**: New system reduces time-to-value by 60%
- **Community Building**: 5,000+ active members in the Memoro forum
- **Support Response**: Average response time under 2 hours
- **Retention Champion**: 85% of supported enterprise customers renew

## The Emma Method

Emma's approach is based on four pillars:

1. **Empathy first**: "Understand the problem behind the problem"
2. **Proactive over reactive**: Solve problems before they arise
3. **Education over operation**: Empower users, don't create dependencies
4. **Celebrate Success**: Celebrate every user success
