## Full Control Over Your App

Memoro's advanced settings allow you to customize the app exactly to your workflow and preferences.

### Personalization

#### 🎨 Appearance
- Dark Mode / Light Mode
- Color schemes
- Font sizes
- Compact or expanded view

#### 🔊 Audio Settings
- Choose default microphone
- Recording quality (High/Medium/Low)
- Noise reduction
- Automatic muting

#### 🌍 Language & Region
- App language
- Default recording language
- Date and time format
- Currency for billing

### Advanced Options

#### 🔐 Privacy & Security
- Biometric authentication
- Auto-lock timer
- Data export settings
- Anonymization options

#### 🔄 Synchronization
- Enable/disable cloud backup
- Sync intervals
- Offline mode
- Limit data usage

#### 🧠 AI Preferences
- Summary detail level
- Technical vocabulary recognition
- Sentiment analysis on/off
- Custom prompts

### Automation

- **Auto-Upload**: Automatically process new recordings
- **Smart Tags**: Automatic tag suggestions
- **Notifications**: Customizable reminders
- **Shortcuts**: Custom keyboard combinations

With advanced settings, Memoro becomes your perfectly customized productivity app.
