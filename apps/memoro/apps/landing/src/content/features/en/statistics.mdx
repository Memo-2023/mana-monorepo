## Keep Track of Your Productivity

Memoro's statistics feature gives you detailed insights into your recording habits and helps you make better use of your time.

### Key Metrics

#### 📝 Memos
- Total number of all recordings
- Average memos per day/week/month
- Trends and developments

#### 💡 Memories
- Number of generated AI summaries
- Quality and depth of analyses
- Most common topics and categories

#### ⏱️ Recording Duration
- Total recording time
- Average memo length
- Longest and shortest recordings

#### 📊 Words
- Total word count of all transcripts
- Speaking speed
- Vocabulary statistics

### Advanced Analytics

- **Time Distribution**: When do you record the most?
- **Tag Analysis**: Which topics dominate?
- **Productivity Trends**: How is your usage developing?
- **ROI Calculation**: How much time are you saving?

### Benefits for You

- **Self-Reflection**: Better understand your work style
- **Optimization**: Identify improvement potential
- **Motivation**: See your progress
- **Planning**: Make data-driven decisions

With Memoro statistics, you always have your productivity under control and can continuously improve your workflow.
