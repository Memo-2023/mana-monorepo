### Available Blueprints

#### 📊 Meeting Analysis
- Automatic agenda point detection
- Decision and action item extraction
- Participant-specific summaries
- Follow-up tasks

#### 🎤 Interview
- Question-answer structuring
- Key statement extraction
- Quote highlights
- Thematic grouping

#### 💡 Brainstorming
- Idea collection and grouping
- Creativity metrics
- Concept development
- Next steps

#### 📝 Customer Feedback
- Sentiment analysis
- Problem identification
- Improvement suggestions
- Priority matrix

### How It Works

1. **Choose Blueprint**: Select the appropriate template before recording
2. **Record**: Speak normally - Memoro focuses on relevant aspects
3. **Optimized Processing**: AI analyzes specifically according to blueprint criteria
4. **Perfect Results**: Get exactly the structure you need

### Benefits

- **Time Saving**: No more post-processing needed
- **Consistency**: Always same quality and structure
- **Focus**: Concentrate on the conversation, not on notes
- **Professional**: Impress with perfectly structured documents
