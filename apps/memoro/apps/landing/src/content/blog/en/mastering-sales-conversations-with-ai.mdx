## The 24-Hour Dilemma in Sales

The statistics are sobering: **78% of all deals go to the vendor who responds first.** Not the cheapest. Not the best. The fastest.

But here's the problem:

**The traditional sales workflow:**
```
Customer meeting (45 min)
    ↓
Type up notes (20 min)
    ↓
Create CRM entry (15 min)
    ↓
Write follow-up email (25 min)
    ↓
Create proposal (60+ min)
───────────────────────────
= At least 2.5 hours until follow-up
```

In this time, your competitor has already sent three follow-ups, created a tailored proposal, and closed the deal.

## Why Sales Teams Need AI Protocols

An average sales rep conducts 15-20 conversations per week. At 20 minutes of follow-up per conversation, that's **6-7 hours of pure documentation work** – almost a full workday that you're not selling.

**What happens during this time?**
- Leads go cold
- Competitors overtake you
- Details are forgotten
- Momentum is lost
- Close rates decline

The solution? Automated conversation documentation that gives you this time back.

## Practice Guide: AI Protocols in Daily Sales

### Step 1: Prepare the First Conversation

**Before the call:**
1. Create a brief conversation agenda
2. Define your main goal (info, qualification, close?)
3. Start recording with a context tag

**Example start:**
*"Meeting with Schneider LLC, first contact, goal: needs analysis, date: January 28, 2025"*

This context helps the AI with later categorization.

### Step 2: During the Conversation

**The golden rules:**
- 🎯 **Active listening** instead of taking notes
- 🎯 **Ask questions** when unclear (everything gets documented)
- 🎯 **Summarize** at the end of the conversation for clarity
- 🎯 **Next steps** explicitly agree upon

**Tip:** Use open questions that help the AI later:
- "What are your biggest challenges currently?"
- "What would a solution need to do for you to say: perfect?"
- "What concerns do you still have?"

### Step 3: After the Conversation – The 15-Minute Workflow

```
Minute 1-2:   Skim transcript
              ↓
Minute 3-5:   Mark/tag important sections
              ↓
Minute 6-10:  Write personalized follow-up
              ↓
Minute 11-13: CRM update with AI insights
              ↓
Minute 14-15: Schedule next steps in calendar
```

**Result:** Professional follow-up within 1 hour instead of 1 day.

### Step 4: Follow-up & Relationship Building

**The 24-48-72 System:**

```
24 hours:  Personal thank you email with recap
           └─ Contains specific conversation points

48 hours:  Promised materials/information
           └─ With reference to specific questions

72 hours:  Check-in & next step
           └─ "Did you have time to look at X?"
```

With AI protocols, this system is effortlessly implementable.
