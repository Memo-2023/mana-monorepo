# Social Media Posts

The Social Media Posts Memory transforms your conversation into viral, platform-appropriate social media content.

## Content Strategies

### Hashtag Optimization
- **Trending**: Current trend hashtags
- **Niche**: Specific community tags
- **Branded**: Own brand hashtags
- **Mix**: 30% popular, 70% specific

### Posting Times
- **LinkedIn**: Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM
- **Twitter**: Mon-Fri, 9 AM & 5 PM
- **Instagram**: 11 AM-1 PM & 7-9 PM
- **Facebook**: Wed-Fri, 1-4 PM
- **TikTok**: 6-9 AM & 7-11 PM

### Engagement Boosters
- Ask questions
- Include polls
- Tag users
- Use Stories/Reels
- User-generated content

## Tips for Optimal Results

1. **Mark highlight moments**
   - "This would be perfect for social media"
   - "Quote-worthy: ..."
   - "We have to share this"

2. **Describe visuals**
   - "This could be visualized well"
   - "Perfect for an infographic"
   - "Story idea: ..."

3. **Emphasize key messages**
   - Repeat main points
   - Mention numbers and facts
   - Highlight successes

4. **Include storytelling**
   - Tell anecdotes
   - Give examples
   - Include emotions
