## Overview

July 2025 marks an important milestone in Memoro's history: We've surpassed **2,500 users** and transcribed an impressive **45 million words**. With over **13,650 recordings** and **221,648 memo entries**, the intensive use of our platform is evident.

## User Development

### Growth and Activity
With **387 new users** in July, we recorded 18.1% growth compared to the previous month. **602 users** were active in July (23.9%) - a solid value that offers room for further engagement.

### Usage Intensity
- **22.7 recordings** per active user per month
- **522 minutes** average recording duration per user
- **368.3 memo entries** per active user

## Recording Statistics

The **314,059 minutes** (over 5,234 hours) of processed recordings correspond to more than **218 days** of continuous audio recordings. The average recording length of **23 minutes** shows that Memoro is particularly used for longer conversations, meetings, and lectures.

### Recordings by Category
- Meetings & Discussions: 38%
- Lectures & Seminars: 24%
- Personal Notes: 19%
- Interviews: 12%
- Other: 7%

## Technical Milestones

### Desktop Apps
The launch of our native desktop apps for Windows and macOS was a complete success:
- **42% of users** have already installed the desktop app
- Significantly improved performance for longer recordings
- Offline functionality for on-the-go use

### Team Workspaces
The new team feature enables companies to create shared workspaces:
- 156 teams created
- Average of 8.3 members per team
- Shared recordings and collaborative notes

### Integrations
- **Slack**: Automatic summaries in channels
- **Google Meet**: One-click meeting recording
- **Microsoft Teams**: Beta phase successfully completed

## AI Feature Usage

With over **1.8 million Mana consumed**, the intensive use of our AI features is evident:

### Mana Consumption by Feature
- AI Summaries: 35%
- Transcriptions: 28%
- Smart Search: 18%
- Translations: 12%
- Advanced Analytics: 7%

### Most Popular AI Functions
1. **Automatic Meeting Minutes**: Structured summaries with action items
2. **Multi-Language Transcription**: Support for 23 languages
3. **Intelligent Search**: Semantic search across all recordings
