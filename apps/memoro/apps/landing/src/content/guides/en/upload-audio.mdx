In Memoro, you can easily upload audio files and have them automatically transcribed. This guide shows you the different ways to do this.

## 📂 Accessing the Upload Feature

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-8 md:gap-12 items-center my-12">
  <div class="order-2 md:order-1">
    <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mb-4">Two ways to upload:</h3>
    <ol class="space-y-3">
      <li><strong>Via Main Menu</strong> - Click the three-dots menu in the top right corner of the app</li>
      <li><strong>Via Memos Page</strong> - Tap the audio archive icon (cloud with arrow) at the top</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
  <div class="order-1 md:order-2 flex justify-center items-center">

  </div>
  <div>
    <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mb-4">Configure your audio file:</h3>
    <ol class="space-y-3">
      <li><strong>Select File</strong> - Supported formats: MP3 or M4A</li>
      <li><strong>Select Language</strong> - Auto-detect or set manually</li>
      <li><strong>Select Date</strong> - Current date or adjust</li>
      <li><strong>Select Time</strong> - Automatic or adjust</li>
      <li><strong>Select Blueprint (optional)</strong> - For optimized processing</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
</div>

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

<div class="bg-background-card border border-border rounded-2xl p-6 my-8">
  <ul class="space-y-3">
    <li><strong>Good Audio Quality</strong> - Ensure clear recordings for best transcription results</li>
    <li><strong>Processing Time</strong> - For longer recordings, processing may take a few minutes</li>
    <li><strong>Continue Working</strong> - You can continue using the app during upload</li>
    <li><strong>Supported Formats</strong> - MP3 and M4A work best</li>
