## The Innovation from Konstanz

Local founders Tobias Müller and Till Schneider have developed Memoro, an app that saves time and changes lives. The Südkurier reported on their impressive success.

## Awarded the Mindelsee Scholarship

The **Mindelsee Scholarship worth up to 100,000 euros** from the "Entrepreneurs for Founders" (UfG) initiative went to Memoro - a confirmation of the app's enormous potential.

## Diverse Use Cases

The app revolutionizes various industries:
- **Healthcare**: Documentation during visits via voice recording
- **Crafts**: From electrical installation to chimney inspection
- **Meetings**: Automatic minutes - concise enough to read, detailed enough for all important information

## The Measurable Benefit

"A caregiver has to spend an average of eight hours per week on documentation. Memoro could reduce that by half," the founders calculate. This saved time directly benefits the patients.

## Technology with Responsibility

The app uses state-of-the-art AI technology but is fully GDPR-compliant - an important factor when dealing with sensitive patient and customer data.
