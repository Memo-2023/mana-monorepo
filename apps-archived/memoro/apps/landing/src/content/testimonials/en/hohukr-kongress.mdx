## Rarely experienced such quick value

_From a 5-star App Store review on December 2nd_

At an eight-hour conference with six presentations and partly catastrophic acoustics, Memoro proved its worth. Where my ears reached their limits, the AI analyzed every presentation meaningfully and documented everything cleanly.

## The perfect conference companion

The challenge at conferences is often poor audio quality - bad microphones, background noise, unclear speakers. Despite these adverse conditions, Memoro accurately documented all six presentations and extracted the key points.

## What convinced me

- **Significantly better than my ears** - even with catastrophic acoustics
- **All presentations meaningfully analyzed** - no important points missed
- **Cleanly documented** - structured summaries instead of chaotic notes
- **Monthly cancellable** - fair terms without cost traps

Rarely have I experienced such immediate value from a new tool. Thumbs up!
