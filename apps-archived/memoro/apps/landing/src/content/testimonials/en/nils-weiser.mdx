## Growing Conviction

At first I was skeptical - another tool? But the longer I use Memoro, the more it convinces me. What started as an experiment has become indispensable. I now document literally everything with it.

## What I Capture

As CTO of Memoro GmbH, I use Memoro for:
- **All Sprint Plannings** - Nothing gets lost anymore, all commitments are clear
- **Daily Standups** - Blockers and progress automatically documented
- **Tech Decisions** - Why did we choose this architecture?
- **1-on-1s with the team** - Development goals and feedback captured in a structured way
- **Stakeholder Meetings** - Requirements and changes in black and white

## Why It Just Makes Sense

- **Complete History** - I can understand months later why we made decisions
- **No more discussions** about what was discussed - everything is documented
- **Perfect Sprint Retrospectives** - All data and facts available
- **Onboarding new devs** - They can search through the complete project history

What convinced me the most: It doesn't cost any extra time. Memoro just runs along and I can fully focus on the conversations. After a few weeks, you don't want to work without it anymore.
