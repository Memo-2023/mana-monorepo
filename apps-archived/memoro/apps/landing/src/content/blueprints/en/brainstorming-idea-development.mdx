# Brainstorming & Idea Development

This blueprint supports creative processes and transforms ideas into concrete implementation plans.

## Key Features

### Compact Session Results
Clear summary of the most important insights and decisions.

### Structured Idea Collection
All concepts sorted and evaluated by feasibility, effort, and impact.

### Concrete Next Steps
Clear task distribution for implementing prioritized ideas.

### Knowledge Management
Creation of content for internal knowledge management or intranet.

## Used Prompts

1. **Executive Summary**: Compact session results
2. **Ideas & Suggestions**: All concepts sorted by feasibility
3. **Tasks & Appointments**: Next steps for implementation
4. **Blog Post**: For internal knowledge management or intranet

## Use Cases

- Design Thinking workshops
- Strategy development
- Product development
- Innovation sessions
- Problem-solving workshops
- OKR planning
- Creative meetings

## Tips for Optimal Results

1. Use the "Yes, and..." technique
2. Let all ideas remain unjudged initially
3. Use timeboxing for idea phases
4. Visualize concepts during recording
5. Cluster similar ideas
6. Evaluate using clear criteria
7. Define concrete pilot projects
