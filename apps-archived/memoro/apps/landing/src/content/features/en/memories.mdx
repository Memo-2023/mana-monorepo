- **Explorative Memories**
  - 💡 Insights: Key points summarized chronologically
  - 📝 Summary: Automatic formatting as minutes, presentation, or form
  - 📄 Article: Structured content preparation

- **Executive Memories**
  - ✅ Tasks: Captures assignees, deadlines, and status
  - 📅 Appointments: Collects date, location, and description

- **Origin of Memories**
  - 🎯 Transcript: Word-for-word record with speaker recognition
  - 🎙️ Audio Recording: Foundation for processing and later verification
