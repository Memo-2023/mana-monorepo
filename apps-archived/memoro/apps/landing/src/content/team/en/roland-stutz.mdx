## The Financial Architect

Roland Stutz is the CFO who orchestrates Memoro's sustainable growth. With his unique blend of startup experience and financial expertise, he builds a company that is both profitable and human.

## Transparency as a Principle

After successful stints at two SaaS unicorns and his own exit, Roland brings his vision of radical financial transparency to Memoro. "Numbers tell stories - and at Memoro, everyone should understand this story."

### His Innovations

- **Open Dashboard**: Real-time metrics for all employees
- **Sustainable Growth**: LTV:CAC of 4:1 as minimum
- **Team Equity**: 100% employee participation
- **Finance Education**: Workshops for all team members

## Growth with Values

"We're proving that sustainable growth and profitability aren't opposites. At Memoro, we're building a company for the next decades, not the next quarterly report."

With his philosophy of "People over Profits" and his focus on long-term value, Roland makes Memoro a financially healthy company that never loses sight of its values.
