Florian König is an experienced sales professional who supports Memoro as a freelancer in building partnerships and sales strategy. With over 15 years of B2B sales experience and a special focus on marketing automation and SaaS solutions, he brings valuable expertise to the team.

## The Sales Professional with a Green Heart

What makes Florian special: He combines successful business development with a sustainable lifestyle. From his farm in Pfinztal, where he practices sustainable agriculture, he manages sales partnerships for innovative tech companies. This unique perspective – the connection between digital business and down-to-earth sustainability – also shapes his work at Memoro.

"The best business relationships grow like good plants: with patience, the right care, and a sustainable approach," describes Florian his philosophy. This attitude is reflected in his engagement with organizations like Greenpeace and the World Wildlife Fund.
