Memo Combination allows you to merge related recordings into a single, comprehensive document. Perfect for multi-part meetings or project summaries.

### How It Works

1. **Selection**: Choose the memos you want to combine
2. **Arrangement**: Determine the order of content
3. **Unification**: Memoro creates a new, consolidated memo with all content
4. **AI Optimization**: AI intelligently summarizes all content into a coherent unit

### Use Cases

- **Multi-part Meetings**: Unite pre and post-meeting discussions
- **Project Documentation**: Combine all project-related notes
- **Workshop Series**: Summarize multi-day events
- **Research**: Consolidate various information sources

### Benefits

- **Time Saving**: No more manual merging needed
- **Consistency**: Uniform formatting and structure
- **Completeness**: Nothing gets lost, everything in one place
- **Intelligence**: AI recognizes connections and eliminates redundancies
