## Your Central Audio Library

The Memoro Audio Archive is your personal control center for all voice recordings. Get a complete overview of your entire audio history at a glance.

### Key Features

- **Complete Overview**: See all your recordings at a glance with date, duration, and status
- **Detailed Statistics**: Total number of recordings, cumulative recording time, and processing status
- **Quick Search**: Find any recording instantly by title, date, or tags
- **Local Storage**: All audio files are securely stored on your device

### Perfect For

- **Power Users**: Keep track even with hundreds of recordings
- **Project Managers**: Organize recordings by projects and time periods
- **Compliance**: Meet documentation requirements with complete history

With the Audio Archive, you'll never miss important recordings again and have your entire audio documentation at your fingertips.
