# About Victoria Brückner

Victoria is the driving force behind Memoro's product vision. With her unique ability to build bridges between technology, business, and user needs, she has been instrumental in making Memoro what it is today.

## The Journey to Memoro

After completing her Master's in Business Informatics at TU Berlin, Victoria started her career at an EdTech startup, where she discovered her passion for products that help people unlock their potential. Through positions at Spotify (Product Manager for Podcast Features) and Notion (Senior PM for Collaboration Tools), she gained valuable experience in building scalable product organizations.

"At Memoro, I found the perfect challenge: shaping a product that changes how people interact with their thoughts and ideas. This is product management in its purest form."

## Product Philosophy

Victoria's approach is based on three pillars:

1. **Users first**: Every decision starts with "What value does this create for our users?"
2. **Data informs, intuition guides**: Metrics are important, but not everything can be measured
3. **Iterate to Innovate**: Quick experiments instead of months of planning

## Achievements at Memoro

- Introduction of the revolutionary "Mana System" based on user research
- Increased user retention by 45% through targeted feature development
- Built a product team from 0 to 12 people
- Established a data-driven product culture
- Launched 3 major features, all exceeding their adoption goals
