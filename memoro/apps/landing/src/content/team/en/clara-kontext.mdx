Clara Kontext is Memoro's living memory. As Chief Context Officer, she ensures that no valuable knowledge is lost and that every team member has the perfect context for their work.

## The Master of Connections

"Context is everything," Clara often says. "A brilliant idea without context is like a puzzle piece without a puzzle - worthless." With her unique ability to recognize patterns and make connections, she transforms isolated information into a vibrant knowledge network.

Her journey began with a Master's in Knowledge Management and Information Architecture. After formative years as a documentarian at the UN and Knowledge Manager at McKinsey, she found her true calling at Memoro: being an organization's collective memory.

## The Clara Method

Her approach is as systematic as it is creative:

### Living Documentation
For Clara, documentation isn't a static archive but a living organism:
- **Context Interviews**: Weekly conversations with all teams
- **Story Mining**: Extracts important learnings from daily work
- **Knowledge Graphs**: Visualizes connections between projects
- **Temporal Mapping**: Shows how decisions evolve

### The Impact

Clara's work shows measurable results:
- **70% faster onboarding** through perfect contextualization
- **0% knowledge loss** during team transitions
- **45% fewer meetings** through better preparation
- **100% documented decisions** with complete history

## Context Coffee & Culture

Clara's famous "Context Coffee" sessions are legendary. Every Friday, she hosts informal conversations where stories are shared and connections are made. "The best insights emerge between the lines," she knows.

### Her Tools
- **Obsidian**: For networked documentation
- **Memoro**: Of course! For all interviews and conversations
- **Miro**: For visual knowledge maps
- **Custom AI**: Self-trained models for pattern recognition

## The Philosophy Behind It

Three principles guide Clara's work:

1. **"Everything is connected"**: No information exists in isolation
2. **"Context beats content"**: The framework makes information valuable
3. **"Forgetting is expensive"**: Every lost learning costs time and money

## Personal Side

Beyond the knowledge networks:
- **Collecting passion**: Old company chronicles and diaries
- **Memory Palace**: Practices classical memory techniques
- **Storytelling**: Performs at Story Slams in Berlin
- **Wall art**: Her office wall displays the complete Memoro timeline

## Her Vision

"At Memoro, we help people never forget anything. As Chief Context Officer, I ensure this applies to us as a company too. Every decision, every learning, every story - everything has its place in our collective memory."

With her unique blend of analytical thinking, narrative intelligence, and genuine interest in people, Clara makes Memoro an organization that not only learns from its past but actively uses it to shape the future.
