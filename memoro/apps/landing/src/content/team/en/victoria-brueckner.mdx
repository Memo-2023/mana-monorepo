Victoria Brückner is the architect behind Memoro's product vision. As Head of Product, she combines strategic thinking with deep user understanding to create products that truly move people forward.

## The Bridge Builder

Her surname says it all: Victoria builds bridges - between what's technically possible and what users really need. Between different teams that all speak their own language. Between tomorrow's vision and the steps that need to be taken today.

She has perfected this ability over years: After her Master's in Business Informatics, she gained experience at some of Europe's most innovative tech companies. At Spotify, she developed features for millions of podcast listeners. At Notion, she helped redefine team collaboration.

## Her Mission at Memoro

"When I first saw Memoro, I knew: This is it. A product that doesn't just solve a problem, but changes how people think and work," Victoria recalls her first days with the team.

Since then, she has been instrumental in shaping Memoro:

- **The Mana System**: Her biggest innovation - a credit system that perfectly balances fairness and flexibility
- **User Research at Scale**: Building a system that continuously translates user feedback into product decisions
- **45% Higher Retention**: Through targeted feature development based on behavioral analysis
- **Product-Market Fit**: Iterative product adaptation to perfect market fit

## The Victoria Approach

Victoria's product philosophy can be summarized in three principles:

1. **"Show me the user pain"**: Every feature starts with a real user problem
2. **"Data tells, stories sell"**: Numbers inform decisions, but user stories drive them
3. **"Ship to learn"**: Better an MVP today than the perfect solution tomorrow

## On the Team

Colleagues appreciate Victoria for:
- Her legendary "Product Review Sessions" - structured but never boring
- The ability to translate complex roadmaps into understandable visions
- Her open-door policy - anyone can come to her with ideas
- The "Victoria Test": Can she explain the feature to her grandma? If not, it's too complicated

## Tools & Methods

Victoria swears by:

- **OKRs** for clear goal setting
- **Continuous Discovery** for constant learning

With her unique blend of strategic thinking, user empathy, and execution strength, Victoria is the perfect product leader for Memoro's ambitious mission.
