# Executive Summary

The Executive Summary Memory provides you with an ultra-fast overview of your conversation in just 3-5 sentences.

## Key Features

### TLDR Format
Your conversation is reduced to the absolute core points - perfect for a quick overview.

### Action-Focused
Only the most important points relevant for decisions or next steps are highlighted.

### Time Saving
Capture the essence of a multi-hour conversation in seconds.

## Use Cases

- Quick decision-making after meetings
- Executive briefings for leadership
- Sharing key messages with uninvolved colleagues
- Preparation for follow-up appointments
- Archiving important conversation content

## Tips for Optimal Results

1. **Clear structure in conversation**: The more structured your conversation, the more precise the summary
2. **Emphasize key messages**: Repeat important points in the conversation for better capture
3. **Make decisions explicit**: Clearly formulate decisions and next steps
4. **Timely processing**: Create the summary as soon as possible after the conversation

## Sample Output

> **Development Team Meeting (45 min)**
> 
> The new API interface will be completed by the end of March, enabling 40% faster data processing. The budget has been increased by €15,000 to hire two additional freelancers for the critical phase. The next milestone has been set for February 15th for the first prototype, with the testing team being involved immediately.

## Workflow Integration

The Executive Summary combines perfectly with other memories:
- Use it as an introduction, followed by the detailed summary for specifics
- Combine with the Tasks memory for concrete to-dos
- Supplement with open questions for follow-up conversations
